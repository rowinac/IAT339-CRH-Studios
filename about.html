About CRH Studios

As a small business based in Vancouver, BC, CRH Studios curates timeless ceramic pieces that embrace the elegance of a home created by our two in-house artists, Tom Crew and ORIENTO. We strive to deliver the highest quality creations that cater to the tastes of our clients, yet with their handmade nature, CRH Studios highlights the quirks and personality of each individual item to emphasize its uniqueness and originality.

Tom Crew

THE ARTIST

Designed and based in Vancouver, BC, Tom Crew’s work lends a sense of natural elements and modernity that serves as functional pieces for the table or home. Tom found his passion with pottery in 1972, where he explored the medium in self-directed practice. Currently, Tom has his ceramics featured at the Princessehof Ceramics Museum in Leeuwarden, one of the most prestigious museums for presenting historical and contemporary ceramics all of size and shape. Specializing in porcelain, Tom has the skill to manipulate clay into clean and modern practical mugs, vases, bottles, and much more. Tom continues to create beautiful, modest products - ones that bring joy to everyday life, created with ingenuity and respect for tradition.

ORIENTO

THE ARTIST

Originally from Jiangxi, China, ORIENTO merges her oriental roots with colorful, eccentric influences of Venice, Italy where she's lived and worked since 2002 until she moved to Vancouver, BC in 2015. ORIENTO's art celebrates vitality and flow, capturing the energy of different cultures in her pieces. Her work has appeared in exhibitions across the globe, including Barcelona, Boston, and Jiangxi. She excels at handcrafted ceramic sculptures, product design, and has a vast knowledge of ceramic engineering which helps her sculpt unique, unconventional, and distinctive ceramics. ORIENTO continues to captivate through her storytelling created from her ceramics.
